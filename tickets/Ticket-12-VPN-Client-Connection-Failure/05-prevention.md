## Prevention Steps
- Document VPN client permission requirements.
- Inform users that VPN activation requires administrative access.
- Establish clear escalation paths for VPN access requests.

## Operational Notes
- VPN issues are often client-side and permission-based.
- Avoid granting local administrator rights unless required.

## Escalation Criteria
- VPN access required by non-administrative users
- Requests to modify VPN client permissions
